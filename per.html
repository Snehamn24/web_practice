The Brave Monster Hunter from *The Sea Beast*

About Maisie

Maisie Brumble is a courageous young girl from the animated film The Sea Beast. Known for her fearless spirit, determination, and compassionate heart, Maisie dreams of becoming a legendary monster hunter—just like the heroes she read about in her books.

Her Story

Growing up in an orphanage, Maisie was inspired by tales of hunters who sailed the seas battling great beasts. She later joins Jacob Holland on the Inevitable ship, forming a powerful bond with a “monster” named Red, proving that understanding is stronger than fear.

Why Maisie Inspires Us

  • She is brave and adventurous
  • She fights for truth and peace
  • She stands up for what she believes in
  • She changes the world with kindness
